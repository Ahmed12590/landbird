About Us

Welcome to Landbird Immigration, a premier immigration consultancy dedicated to providing comprehensive and personalized immigration services. Based in Canada, our firm has been a trusted partner for individuals and families seeking to navigate the complexities of immigration law with ease and confidence.

Since our inception in 2010, Landbird Immigration has been committed to upholding the highest standards of integrity and professionalism. Our team of experienced consultants is dedicated to delivering practical and effective solutions tailored to the unique needs of each client.

Our multilingual team is fluent in Punjabi, Hindi, Urdu, French, Italian, Spanish, Vietnamese, Albanian, Bengali, Portuguese, Cantonese, Gujarati, Mandarin, Kannada, Telugu, Tamil, Dari, Persian/Farsi, and Russian, ensuring that we can effectively communicate with clients from diverse backgrounds and provide the support they need in their preferred language.
