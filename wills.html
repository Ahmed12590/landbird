Wills, Estates, and Trusts

Alternative Dispute Resolution
We provide efficient and amicable solutions for resolving disputes outside the courtroom. Our alternative dispute resolution services save time and reduce stress by facilitating negotiations and mediation to achieve mutually beneficial outcomes.
Advice to Executors, Trustees & Beneficiaries
We offer professional advice to executors, trustees, and beneficiaries, helping them navigate their roles and responsibilities. Our expertise ensures that they fulfill their duties effectively and in compliance with legal requirements.
Consent & Capacity Issues
Our specialized team assists in matters of consent and capacity, ensuring that decisions are made legally and ethically. We provide support in assessing capacity and handling related legal issues with sensitivity and precision.
Dependant Support Claims
We help dependants secure the support they need from an estate. Our team works diligently to ensure fair and adequate provision for those who were financially dependent on the deceased.
Estate & Trust Litigation
Our robust legal representation in estate and trust disputes protects your rights and interests. We handle all aspects of litigation, from initial consultation to resolution, ensuring your case is managed professionally.
Estate Litigation
We specialize in resolving conflicts arising from estate matters. Our experienced litigators provide strong representation to safeguard your interests and achieve favorable outcomes.
Estate Trustees
We offer comprehensive services for estate trustees, ensuring smooth administration and compliance with legal obligations. Our guidance helps trustees manage their duties effectively and efficiently.
Family Law Act Claims
Our team assists with claims under the Family Law Act, ensuring that your entitlements and rights are protected. We provide expert legal advice and representation to navigate these complex matters.
Guardianship Disputes
We resolve disputes over guardianship with sensitivity and legal expertise. Our services ensure that the best interests of the individual are upheld, and conflicts are resolved amicably.
Guardianships & Capacity
We provide guidance and support in establishing and managing guardianships, and addressing capacity issues effectively. Our team ensures that the legal and personal needs of the individual are met with care and diligence.
Intervivos Gifts or Wealth Transfer
We offer strategic planning for the transfer of wealth during your lifetime. Our services ensure that your gifts and wealth transfers are executed smoothly and in a tax-efficient manner.
Living Will
We assist in crafting a living will that reflects your healthcare preferences. Our team ensures that your wishes are clearly documented and respected, providing peace of mind for you and your family.
Mediation Services for Resolving Estates and Trust Disputes
Our mediation services facilitate peaceful resolutions to estate and trust disputes. We help parties reach agreements that preserve relationships and avoid the costs and stress of litigation.
Notary Public
Our professional notary services authenticate important documents, providing legal validation and assurance. We ensure that your documents are properly notarized and meet all legal requirements.
Passing of Accounts
We assist executors and trustees in the formal accounting process. Our services ensure transparency, accuracy, and compliance with legal standards, providing peace of mind for all parties involved.
Power of Attorney
We help you establish a power of attorney to manage your affairs in case of incapacity. Our services ensure that your chosen representative has the authority to act on your behalf when needed.
Power of Attorney Disputes
We resolve conflicts related to the execution of a power of attorney. Our legal expertise ensures that disputes are handled efficiently and fairly, protecting your interests.
Power of Attorney for Property & Personal Care
We assist in setting up a power of attorney for property and personal care. Our services ensure that your financial and personal care decisions are managed according to your wishes.
Probate & Estate Administration
We provide efficient and effective administration of estates through the probate process. Our team handles all aspects of probate, ensuring that your loved one's estate is settled smoothly and in accordance with the law.
Solicitor’s Negligence
We offer legal recourse for instances of solicitor’s negligence in estate matters. Our team ensures that you receive fair compensation and that your interests are protected.
Trust Disputes
We address and resolve disputes related to trusts with experienced legal support. Our services ensure that trust-related conflicts are managed professionally and effectively.
Trust Interpretation
We provide clear interpretation of trust documents to ensure their proper implementation. Our expertise helps clarify the terms of the trust and resolve any ambiguities.
Trustee/Executor Disputes
We offer legal assistance in resolving conflicts between trustees and executors. Our services ensure that disputes are handled efficiently and in the best interests of the estate.
Will Challenges & Defending a Will
